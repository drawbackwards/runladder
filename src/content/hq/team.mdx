## Engineering reality

There is no dedicated human engineer on the team today. Engineering is Ward + Claude (Sara) as AI pair + Sean for weekly audit. This is intentional for now and consistent with Ladder's agent-native positioning. See [Decisions Log → Engineering model](/hq/decisions#engineering-model-current-state).

The gap will tighten first on Pulse pipeline scale, then on API uptime as developer adoption grows. Hiring trigger is tracked on the [Roadmap](/hq/roadmap).

## Day-to-day

**Standup.** Async via Slack. Each owner posts what they shipped yesterday, what they're shipping today, and any blocker. Michael runs the cadence.

**Weekly review.** Wednesdays. 30 minutes. Goes through the [Roadmap](/hq/roadmap) board, surfaces blockers, locks the next week's priorities.

**PR review.** Engineering PRs go through Sean for one-hour weekly review. Non-blocking PRs (typos, copy fixes, content) merge after a one-emoji approval from any teammate.

**Escalation path.** Michael → Ward. Don't go around Michael unless it's a production fire on a surface he doesn't own.

## Updating this page

Every roster or surface-ownership change is a PR to `src/content/hq/team.mdx`. The "Updated" line at the top updates with the PR. If the page hasn't been touched in 30 days, the [Roadmap](/hq/roadmap) weekly review flags it.
